Frequently Asked Questions

How long can the drone fly?

Most customer flights last around 5 minutes, with up to 15 minutes of operational flight time.

How long does it take to recharge?

Recharging takes approximately 2x the flight time. A 5-minute flight takes 10 minutes to reach full charge.

Do I need a pilot?

While fully autonomous, most regions require a pilot-in-command to be connected during flight operations.
